#publications

"Certificate Generation and Validation Using Blockchain," TIJER International Research Journal, Vol. 11, Issue 4, April 2024.
DOI: Click here to view the publication

In this paper, we proposed a blockchain-based system for generating and validating digital certificates. The approach ensures enhanced security, transparency, and immutability, addressing common issues like forgery and unauthorized alterations. This solution provides an efficient and reliable method for certificate issuance and verification in various sectors, including education and professional certifications.
